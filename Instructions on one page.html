What each letter (and number) means in algorithms:
U: rotate the top of the cube
B: rotate the back of the cube
F: rotate the front of the cube
R: rotate the right of the cube
L: rotate the left of the cube
C: do the rotation clockwise
CC: do the rotation counterclockwise
2: do the rotation twice in any direction

The side which is facing you is the front of the cube
